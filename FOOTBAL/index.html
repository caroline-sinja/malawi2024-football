News & Updates

In Malawi's 2024 football scene, significant highlights include the Airtel Top 8 Cup, where FCB Nyasa Big Bullets clinched their third title in four seasons with a narrow 1-0 victory over Silver Strikers. Ernest Petro's decisive header in the second half secured this victory, marking a high point for Bullets as they defended their title successfully at Bingu National Stadium​​. Airtel Top 8 Tournament Action: Mighty Mukuru Wanderers and Bullets FC are among the favorites in this year's Airtel Top 8. Civil Service United, eager to break their trophy drought, aim to capitalize on the Top 8 as their first title opportunity since 2015. Wanderers, trophy-less since 2022, hope to end their drought with this competition​​​.

The Blantyre Derby between Bullets and Mighty Mukuru Wanderers was also noteworthy, showcasing intense competition and tactical maneuvers. Wanderers, strengthened by standout players like Felix Zulu and Blessings Singini, showed a much-improved defense this season, conceding just one goal in four matches. Meanwhile, Bullets relied on midfield stability from players like Yankho Singo and Ernest Petro, making them formidable in possession and attack​​​​.

Additionally, Wanderers recorded an impressive 5-0 win over Mighty Wakawaka Tigers in the Super League, keeping them competitive among league leaders​.Historic Game Highlights: Bullets FC set a record in the recent Castel Challenge Cup with a staggering 22-1 victory, showcasing their offensive power and dominance in Malawian football​​.
